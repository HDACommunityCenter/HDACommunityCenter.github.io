About

Welcome to HDACC! We're a community-focused organization dedicated to empowering individuals and families through a variety of programs, events, and resources. The HDA Community Cultural Center was established in 2024 and is located in the Waterloo and Kitchener areas.

Mission:

The HDA Cultural Centre is a community-focused, welcoming and vibrant hub dedicated to enriching the lives of all community members. Through cultural, educational, and wellness programs we foster connection and healthy living, and supporting individuals to thrive in a friendly and healthy environment.

Vision:

The HDA Cultural Centre is where every member of our community finds belonging, purpose, and joy. While celebrating diversity, we nurture social connections and promoting overall wellbeing. We strive to build a caring and dynamic community that uplifts and inspires all.
